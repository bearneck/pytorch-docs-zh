    torch.tensor Constructs a tensor with no autograd history (also known as a "leaf tensor", see Autograd mechanics) by copying data.

    torch.sparse_bsc_tensor Constructs a sparse tensor in BSC (Block Compressed Sparse Column)) with specified 2-dimensional blocks at the given ccol_indices and row_indices.

    torch.as_strided See torch.as_strided()
